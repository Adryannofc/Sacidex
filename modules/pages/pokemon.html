Charizard, the final form of Charmander, is a Fire/Flying type Pokémon. It is one of the most recognizable Pokémon from Generation 1, with its large wings and the ability to breathe intense flames. Charizard's fearsome and majestic appearance, combined with its ability to fly and unleash powerful fire-based attacks, make it a highly sought-after Pokémon for any trainer's team.
